## Notes on Confidence Levels

- **HIGH (85-95%)**: Reliable, multiple sources with consistent pricing
- **MEDIUM (70-80%)**: Good information but some variability
- **LOW (50-60%)**: Limited data or regulatory concerns

## General ROI Considerations

1. Most digital imaging equipment shows ROI within 2-3 years
2. Section 179 tax deductions available for 2024 purchases
3. Certified pre-owned equipment offers 30-50% savings
4. Financing options widely available at competitive rates
5. Technology adoption correlates with practice growth

## Recommendations

1. Consider certified pre-owned for significant savings
2. Bundle purchases for better pricing
3. Evaluate total cost of ownership including maintenance
4. Factor in training time and adoption curve
5. Assess practice volume to determine appropriate investment level
