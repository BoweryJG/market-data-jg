## Key Findings

### Price Ranges by Category
- **RF Body Devices**: $6,000 - $86,000
- **RF Microneedling**: $30,000 - $45,000
- **Picosecond Lasers**: $6,300 - $95,000
- **Vascular/IPL**: $4,000 - $109,000

### Training Costs
- Most manufacturers include basic training with equipment purchase
- Additional/advanced training ranges from free webinars to $2,500 for certification
- Virtual training increasingly common (2-4 hours typical)

### Consumables Impact
- Devices with no consumables: Venus Legacy, BTL Vanquish ME, Accent Prime, BodyFX, Pelleve, Discovery Pico, Stellar M22
- Per-treatment consumables: $50-$250 typical cost
- Handpiece replacement/recharging: $1,250-$1,649

### ROI Considerations
- Treatment pricing ranges: $250-$1,500 per session
- Devices with no consumables achieve faster ROI
- Multi-application platforms (M22, Accent Prime) offer multiple revenue streams
- Used equipment can save 30-70% while maintaining treatment quality

### Purchase Recommendations
1. **For Budget-Conscious Practices**: Consider certified pre-owned equipment
2. **For Maximum Versatility**: Multi-platform devices like Stellar M22 or Accent Prime
3. **For Fastest ROI**: Devices with no consumables
4. **For Specific Treatments**: Specialized devices may offer better results

### Note on Confidence Levels
- **Verified**: Actual prices found from marketplaces or dealers
- **Estimated**: Based on treatment costs, market positioning, or partial information
